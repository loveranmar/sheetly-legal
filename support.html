Common questions

My scan didn't convert well.

Recognition works best with a clear, well-lit photo: lay the page flat, fill the frame with one page at a time, and avoid shadows and glare. Printed scores convert much better than handwritten ones. If a scan keeps failing, email me the photo and I'll take a look.

I bought Sheetly Pro but it isn't showing up.

Try tapping Restore Purchases in the app's settings. If that doesn't work, sign out and back in to your account and try again, or email me with your purchase receipt.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iOS Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Sheetly Pro → Cancel. Cancelling stops the next renewal but you keep Pro until the current period ends. The Lifetime unlock is a one-time purchase — there's nothing to cancel.

Where are my practice recordings stored?

Practice recordings are saved only on your device — they are never uploaded. That also means deleting the app deletes your recordings, so export anything you want to keep first. Your scores, on the other hand, are safely synced to your account.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email me at idk.techz.taken@gmail.com from the address you signed up with and I'll erase your account and everything linked to it. See the Privacy Policy for details on what is stored.
